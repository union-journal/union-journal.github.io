CALL FOR PROPOSALS

UNION is a student-run journal for art and
literature published by The Cooper Union.

UNION is accepting submissions for its second annual issue. We are seeking proposals for projects that will consider alternative forms of digital publication on or surrounding the theme of SANDBOX.

The submission deadline is July 31st.

Traditional forms such as fiction, essay, poetry, and visual art are welcome as well as hybrid forms that work in video, audio, architecture, engineering, and digital design.

Approved proposals will work with the editorial, design, and engineering staff to develop projects for the final digital publication.

UNION is also looking for designers and web developers with any level of experience to join the staff for the upcoming issue.

Email unionjournal@gmail.com if interested.
